Frequently Asked Questions 🤔

Getting Started

What age is Gem Glow Academy designed for?

Gem Glow Academy is designed specifically for girls aged 7-12. All activities, language, and concepts are carefully developed to match this developmental stage. If your daughter is outside this age range, we may still have resources that could help, but the program is optimized for 7-12 year olds.

Do I need to create an account to use Gem Glow Academy?

Creating an account is optional but recommended! An account lets you track progress, earn gems, and unlock achievements across sessions. If you'd rather not create an account, you can still explore the activities, but your progress won't be saved.

Using the App

How long do activities take?

Most activities take between 2-10 minutes. We designed them this way because we know girls are busy! Short activities fit easily into daily schedules, making it realistic to build positive habits. The timer guides you through each activity.

What are the three areas and how do I choose?

The three areas are:

  • Emerald Eden: Focus on building positive daily habits that strengthen you from the inside
  • Sapphire Sanctuary: Explore and manage emotions in a safe, supportive space
  • Ruby Rise: Develop confidence, courage, and leadership skills

You can visit any focus area anytime based on what you need that day. Some days you might focus on habits, other days on emotions or leadership. It's totally up to you!

How do I earn gems and what are they for?

You can earn 1 gem for a completed activity. Your spendable balance is saved to your child profile and can be used in the palace shop.

  • 100 collectible items across furniture, lighting, rugs, plants, art, pets and magic.
  • Five starter items are included with every child profile.
  • No final reward screen: your room and future collections can keep growing.
Can I do activities multiple times?

Absolutely! Activities are designed to be repeated. In fact, repetition is how habits form and skills develop. You can do the same activity multiple times and earn gems each time you complete it. Feel free to revisit your favorites!

Safety & Privacy

Is my data safe and private?

Yes! We take privacy seriously. Your data is encrypted, never sold to third parties, and handled with care. We comply with all child privacy laws including COPPA. Read our full Privacy Policy for details on how we protect your information.

Can parents/guardians monitor progress?

We're developing a parent/guardian dashboard so adults can see progress and support their daughters. For now, feel free to ask your daughter how she's using Gem Glow Academy! We recommend open conversations about the activities and what she's learning.

Is Gem Glow Academy a replacement for professional help?

No. Gem Glow Academy is a tool for personal growth and habit-building. It's not a replacement for therapy, counseling, or professional mental health support. If a girl is experiencing serious emotional struggles, please reach out to a qualified professional. Gem Glow Academy works best alongside professional support when needed.

Support & Feedback

What if I'm not sure which activity to try?

We recommend taking the Mood Check-In quiz when you first visit each area. It asks how you're feeling and suggests activities that match your current state. You can also spin the Activity Roulette wheel to let fate choose for you — sometimes surprise challenges are the most fun!

How can I give feedback or suggest new activities?

We'd love to hear from you! Visit our Support page or use the "Ask a Question" button in the footer. Tell us what you love, what you'd like to see improved, or suggest new activities. Your feedback helps us make Gem Glow Academy better for all girls.

What if I find an activity inappropriate or uncomfortable?

Your comfort matters! If any activity feels wrong or makes you uncomfortable, skip it and try another one. We have tons of options. Please also let us know by contacting Support — we want to hear about it so we can improve.

Who can I contact if I have questions?

Great question! You can reach us via the Support page or by clicking "Ask a Question" in the footer. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. You can also email us directly at hello@gemglowacademy.com or find us on Instagram @gemglowacademy.
