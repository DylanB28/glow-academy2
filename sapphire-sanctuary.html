🧠 Brain-Science Calm Hacks

These simple tricks actually change what's happening in your brain to help you feel calm and in control!

3 min
🌊

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

Notice 5 things you see, 4 you touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste. This pulls your brain back into the present moment.

4 min
💨

Box Breathing

Breathe in for 4, hold for 4, out for 4, hold for 4. This activates your calm nervous system instantly.

2 min
🧊

Cold Water Reset

Splash cold water on your face or hold something cold. This slows your heart rate and resets your nervous system.

🎙️ Voice Journal 🎙️

Sometimes it's easier to talk than write. Record your thoughts and feelings out loud!

💡 Talking about your feelings helps your brain process them. You're doing great!
